What is an LLM?

Introduction

Large Language Models (LLMs) are AI systems trained on massive text datasets to understand and generate human-like language. They can perform tasks like translation, summarization, and creative writing.

Common Use Cases

  • Text generation and completion
  • Chatbots and virtual assistants
  • Content summarization
  • Language translation
  • Sentiment analysis
